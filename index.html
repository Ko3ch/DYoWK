About

Majority of us don't know what day of the week they were born, and by submitting your birthday this app saves you the math steps. Besides that you get 'Ghana-fied'

Ghanian culture has in place a child naming sytem where children are named depending on what day of the week they born. Both male and female names.

Akan Names

From Sunday to Saturday the names are:

Male

Sunday-Kwasi, M-Kwadwo, T-Kwabena, W-Kwaku, TH-Yaw, F-Kofi, ST-Kwame

Female

Sunday-Akosua, M-Adwoa, T-Abenna, W-Akua, TH-Yaa, F-Afua, ST-Ama
